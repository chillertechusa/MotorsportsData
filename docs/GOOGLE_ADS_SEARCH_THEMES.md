## CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE RECOMMENDATIONS

### High-Intent Search Ads
Use themes **1-15** (riders looking for solutions) + **16-27** (mechanics building portfolios) + **28-40** (teams needing tools)
- Bid higher on these
- Direct to tier landing pages (/data/rookie, /data/privateer, /data/mechanic)
- Ad copy: "[Problem] → [Your Solution] → [Real Data Proof]"

### Educational/Awareness Campaigns
Use themes **41-50** (informational, earlier in funnel)
- Bid moderate
- Direct to blog posts or feature pages
- Build audience for remarketing

### Negative Keywords
- "free motocross software" (unless doing free tier push)
- "motocross forum" (people looking for community, not SaaS)
- "motocross game" (confuse your product with gaming)
- "motocross news" (informational, not commercial)
