# Phase 2+ Strategic Backlog

## Deferred / On Hold

### White-Label Multi-Sport Platform Expansion
**Effort:** 3-4 weeks | **Market:** NASCAR, NHRA, drag racing, rally, karting, FMX

**Vision:** Motorsports Data becomes the OS for all two-wheel AND four-wheel racing. Each sport gets a branded overlay; the engine (DB, AI, billing) is identical underneath.

**Implementation:**
- Add `discipline` field to mdVehicles (motocross/sx, enduro, flat-track, fmx, drag, rally, nascar, nhra, karting)
- Multi-branded landing pages per sport (`/nascar`, `/nhra`, `/drag-racing`, etc.)
- Sport-specific AI coaching (drag racing = reaction time focus vs. motocross = corner speed focus)
- Separate pricing tiers per sport (some sports charge more for premium features)
- White-label reseller program (allow brand partners to rebrand MD as their own platform)

**Market Opportunity:** 
- NASCAR: ~200,000 licensed racers + pit crew coaching market
- NHRA: ~100,000 drag racers (premium, high-spend segment)
- Rally/Rallycross: ~50,000 drivers (Europe-focused, high-tech adoption)
- Karting: ~500,000 youth racers (long-tail, team/academy focus)

**Moat:** First platform to unify all motorsports data. Switching cost = entire sport's data library.

**Next: Once white-label is prioritized, start with MVP (1 new sport, 1 white-label partner, proof of concept before full platform expansion)**

## Why Advanced Analytics First

1. **Revenue lever immediately** — Gate analytics behind premium tiers, drives conversion
2. **Retention driver** — Coaches check weekly, reduces churn
3. **Foundation for white-label** — Once analytics work for motocross, scale to NASCAR, NHRA (same engine)
4. **No hardware complexity** — Pure software, 2-week ship
5. **Competitive advantage** — No other racing platform has coach effectiveness metrics
